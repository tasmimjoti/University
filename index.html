Facilities we provide

The university campuses offer learner-friendly environment with so many facilities which all are IT-enabled with a provision for broadband connectivity.

World Class Library

The libraries have a vast collection of books that include fiction, non-fiction, biographies, encyclopaedias, storybooks, picture books, dictionaries and reference books on various subjects along with large number of journals, periodicals, magazines and newspapers, as well as digital resources. We also provide e-library facilities with huge amount of electronic devices like laptops and tablets. All staffs and students have access to free wifi through e-library's devices.

Large Covered Playground

Our each campus has a well-laid, lush green playground, a multi-purpose court and an indoor sports complex. These facilities are used for providing coaching to students in various sports and games such as football, athletics, basketball, lawn tennis, volleyball, table tennis, chess, carrom, etc. Apart from organising regular physical education classes, these facilities are also used for providing specialised coaching to students in various sports and games.

Healthy, hygienic and tasty foods

All the campuses have bright, well ventilated, hygienic space with a colourful lively ambience. The cafeteria serves healthy, nutritious vegetarian snacks and meals with no junk food. All the food items are highly hygienic and are monitored regularly by the authority.Besides, it has Teachers’ Lounge for the faculty members where they can have their snacks and lunch and read newspapers as well. Each cafeteria is air-conditioned and has high speed wifi facility.
