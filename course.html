Our Courses

Courses we offer

We offer several courses for learning, training and research purposes.

Intermediate

This term is almost compulsory for all students in order to become eligible for higher studies. It's a two years course which can be studied after attaining Secondary School Certificate in the field of Engineering, Technology, Medicine, Law, Agriculture, Science and Veterinary Science, Architecture, Education (Teaching) etc.

Degree

We offer both online and offline programs for this category. Work through programs and earn credit towards your degree wherever you are; you just need a smartphone, tablet or computer, and access to the internet. Complete all programs and pass all assessments, to gain an internationally recognised qualification in the fields of Language & Literature, Politics & Society, Nature & Environment, Engineering & Technology, Healthcare & Medicine etc.

Post-graduation

A postgraduate degree encompasses a range of qualifications that require an undergraduate degree to be considered for entry, these include courses at Postgraduate Diploma level all the way to a PhD. Here, you'll find various courses for postgraduation in the field of Applied and Pure Sciences, Business and Management, Computer Science and IT, Engeneering, Health and Medicine and so on.
